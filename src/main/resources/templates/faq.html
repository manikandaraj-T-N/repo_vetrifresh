Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about VetriFresh

How do I place an order?
Browse our shop, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay via UPI, card, or cash on delivery.
What is the delivery time?
We deliver within 24–48 hours of placing your order. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 10 AM.
Are the products organic?
Yes! All our products are sourced from certified organic farms. Products marked with 🌿 are 100% organic.
Can I return or exchange a product?
If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact us within 24 hours. We will arrange a replacement or refund.
How do I track my order?
Log in to your account and visit Order History to track your current orders in real time.
Is there a minimum order value?
The minimum order value is ₹199. Free delivery is available on orders above ₹499.
